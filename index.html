FAQ

Quick answers before you book.

Is Space Karaoke 21+?

Guests should bring physical ID. Strict 21+ policy applies on Friday and Saturday.

Can groups book packages?

Yes. Hourly rooms, bottle packages, open bar, catering, and venue buyouts are available by inquiry.

Can room time be extended?

Extensions depend on availability and can be handled by the team during the visit.
